Chronolabs Cooperative Security Policy

Chronolabs Cooperative uses the PayPal & ANZ Bank Payment Gateway for its online credit card transactions. PayPal & ANZ Bank processes online credit card transactions for thousands of Australian merchants, providing a safe and secure means of collecting payments via the Internet.
All online credit card transactions performed on this site using the PayPal & ANZ Bank gateway are secured payments.

  1. Payments are fully automated with an immediate response.
  2. Your complete credit card number cannot be viewed by Chronolabs Cooperative or any outside party.
  3. All transactions are performed under 128 Bit SSL Certificate.
  4. All transaction data is encrypted for storage within PayPal & ANZ Bank’s bank-grade data center, further protecting your credit card data.
  5. PayPal & ANZ Bank is an authorized third party processor for all the major Australian banks.
  6. PayPal & ANZ Bank at no time touches your funds; all monies are directly transferred from your credit card to the merchant account held by Chronolabs Cooperative.
  7. For more information about PayPal & ANZ Bank and online credit card payments, please visit www.paypal.com or www.anz.com.au
